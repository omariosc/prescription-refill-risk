Risk Tier Reference Calibrated from 180,158 test fills. Boundaries: LOW <0.30 | MODERATE 0.30–0.55 | HIGH ≥0.55

HIGH ≥ 0.55

76% actual late rate

Proactive pharmacist outreach before expected run-out. 33.8% of patients fall here.

MODERATE 0.30–0.55

61% actual late rate

Automated reminder (SMS/email/app) at expected run-out. 33.8% of patients.

LOW < 0.30

40% actual late rate

Standard monitoring. Below population average (59%). 32.4% of patients.

Prediction intervals (90% conformal, MAPIE-inspired) are returned for each prediction. When the interval spans multiple tiers, the uncertain flag is set — the tier assignment should be reviewed by a clinician rather than acted on automatically.
